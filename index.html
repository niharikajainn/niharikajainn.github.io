I'm Niharika Jain, a recent Master's graduate in computer science at Arizona State University, researching gender and racial bias in machine learning.

I am a second-generation American, an immigrant, and a woman of color. I champion diversity in tech and interdisciplinary education. In my undergraduate career, I studied political science, French language and culture, and business data analytics on top of my computer science major. I'm a firm believer that my generation will rely on both computatonal and liberal-arts studies to push society and technology forward, through fairness in AI.

If you don't see me working, you might find me playing music, running, Latin dancing, or Penny boarding. Let's get in touch!
